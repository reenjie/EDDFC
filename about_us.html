Early Disease Detection for Chicken or EDDFC is a web-based application which will be used for the users, or the poultry farm owners detect some diseases for their chicken pets. If there are some noticeable diseases or visually seen diseases, the users would be able to use the website for detecting what kind of disease the chicken may have. There are a lot of known chicken diseases that we may encounter but some of there are caused by parasitic diseases, some are caused by bacteria, and some are visible, and some are not. The detection is only limited to those diseases that are visible. As it would help farm owners monitor the health of their chicken from their farm.
